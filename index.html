Frequently
Asked
Questions

What are the new tokenomics?

The total tax of the Saitama V2 contract is 4% per transaction, where 1% goes to the burn wallet, 1% is redistributed to the pool of holders as reflections and 2% composes the Sustainability tax (of which 1% is in ETH and 1% in Saitama tokens).

Do I risk losing my Saitama tokens?

No, you will not lose your Saitama holdings.
We will take a snapshot of the blockchain showing exactly how much Saitama you held in your wallet in the moment prior to the migration and an equivalent amount of tokens will be airdropped to your wallet under the new contract. When we move liquidity from V1 to V2, these new tokens will show equivalent value in your wallet.

When will I see my new tokens?

The V2 tokens will be airdropped to your wallet, meaning they will be automatically sent without your interference. If you don't see the new tokens after the airdrop automatically, you simply need to add the V2 contract address to your wallet manually and your tokens appear there. Developers will be live during the migration and the Airdrop times will be informed in our official social media channels.

Do I lose money with the supply cut?

No, our supply will reduce but the new amount of tokens you will hold will have the same value as the tokens you had.

We are cutting the supply from 100 Quadrillion to 100 Billion tokens. This means that if you had 1 Trillion tokens in V1 you will have 1 Million tokens in V2, but they will be worth approximate value.

Will moving to V2 cost me anything?

No, you will not have to pay anything. The migration process will be entirely funded by the Saitama Development Team. You will receive your V2 tokens via airdrop free of charge.

What is the new contract address?

The new contract address is: 0xce3f08e664693ca792cace4af1364d5e220827b2, it can be copied from this site or from our official channels. Please DO NOT trust any other sources and DO NOT buy tokens from contracts claiming to be Saitama V2 before double-checking the new address in our official channels.

How does V2 affect exchange listings?

The new tokenomics for V2 were designed to better suit requirements for listing in exchanges of all sizes. As for existing listings nothing will change, we will work with each one of them closely for a smooth migration.

How will this migration differ from other failed migrations in the DeFi space?

Our team has been preparing for the possibility of a V2 for several weeks. We have collectively prepared a detailed migration plan. A snapshot of that migration plan and timeline can be previewed in this document. We have a system in place in which we will airdrop tokens in batches without the need for holders to do anything on their end to receive them. This will be done in the fastest possible way, and the development team is covering all costs to make this process as seamless as possible for our community. Finally, we have set up a support form to help users who have questions or experience issues.

What happens if I'm staking Saitama or holding tokens in a Cold Wallet?

It is recommended that anyone storing their Saitama tokens in a Cold Wallet, such as Ledger, sync and update their position on Etherscan prior to the migration, as our information post-snapshot will be obtained from Etherscan. If you are staking your tokens in third party solutions we recommend you unstake them prior to the snapshot date (TBC).

Will Saitama tokens stored on SaitaMask be migrated to V2?

Yes. SaitaMask is a DeFi wallet, it means the tokens are actually stored in an address on the blockchain. The snapshot will be taken from the blockchain, thus including wallets created and / or imported into SaitaMask.

How do I protect myself from scammers during the migration?

DO NOT trust other channels, the Migration Central and Saitama's official channels will be the only support channels for the migration. DO NOT share your seed phrase (recovery phrase) with anyone. The person holding your seed phrase is holding your tokens. Saitama will NEVER ASK you to share your seed phrase with us. That is not needed for the migration and never will be. If you see any suspicious activity DO NOT interact and please report and help us inform the community.

What is "dusting" and how do I protect myself against it?

There is a possibility that scammers will try to airdrop false V2 tokens to holders' wallets. This is a malicious attack known as "dusting" that can result in the loss of your tokens if you interact with them. When you receive our airdrop, after we inform it was concluded, we recommend you double-check the contract address first before interacting with the tokens, including transferring and swapping them.
